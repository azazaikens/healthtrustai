The Clinician's Charter of Rights

  • Treatment Autonomy

    Make medical decisions without unwarranted interference.

  • Prompt Approvals

    Secure clearance for essential treatments without holdup.

  • Recognition of Knowledge

    Guarantee your expertise and education are respected above budget-driven systems.

  • Appropriate Reimbursement

    Obtain payment for your services without unnecessary postponements.

  • Information Control

    Retain authority over your records, ensuring they benefit you and your patients—not insurance profits.

  • Activism Without Penalty

    Voice support for improvement without concern of consequence.
